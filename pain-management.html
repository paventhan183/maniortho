Pain Management Services

We offer a range of specialized treatments designed to reduce pain and improve function without the need for open surgery.

Chronic Back & Neck Pain Treatment

Comprehensive care for long-standing back and neck pain using a combination of medication, specialized physiotherapy, and targeted injections to reduce inflammation and improve mobility.

Interventional Pain Procedures

Minimally invasive procedures performed to diagnose and treat pain:

  • Epidural Steroid Injections: For relief from sciatica and spinal stenosis pain.
  • Facet Joint Injections: To treat arthritis pain in the spine joints.
  • Nerve Blocks: Targeting specific nerves to block pain signals.
  • Trigger Point Injections: For severe muscle knots and myofascial pain.

Sciatica & Disc Management

Specialized non-surgical management for slipped discs (herniated discs) and sciatica, focusing on reducing nerve irritation and strengthening the spine.

Joint Pain Solutions

Advanced treatments for knee, shoulder, and hip pain, including Viscosupplementation (lubricating fluid injections) and PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy to promote healing.

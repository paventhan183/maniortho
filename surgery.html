Our Surgical Expertise

Dr. Mani Arumugam brings over 12 years of surgical experience, utilizing modern techniques to ensure the best possible outcomes for patients.

Complex Trauma & Fracture Management

Fractures can range from simple hairline cracks to severe compound injuries. We provide comprehensive surgical care for:

  • Peri-articular Fractures: Complex breaks around joints (knee, ankle, elbow) requiring precise anatomical reduction.
  • Long Bone Fractures: Using Interlocking Intramedullary Nailing for femur and tibia fractures to allow early weight bearing.
  • Pelvic & Acetabular Fractures: Specialized management for high-velocity trauma injuries.
  • Open Fractures: Emergency debridement and external fixation to prevent infection and save limbs.
  • Geriatric Fractures: Specialized care for hip fractures in the elderly to ensure quick mobilization.

Joint Replacement (Arthroplasty)

When arthritis severely limits daily activities and pain is unmanageable, joint replacement offers a new lease on life. We utilize minimally invasive techniques for:

  • Total Knee Replacement (TKR): Resurfacing the damaged knee joint with high-quality implants for pain-free walking.
  • Total Hip Replacement (THR): Replacing the hip ball and socket to restore range of motion and stability.
  • Partial Knee Replacement: Treating only the damaged compartment of the knee, preserving natural bone and ligaments.
  • Revision Joint Replacement: Expert care for correcting failed previous implants.

Spine Surgery

Back and neck pain can be debilitating. While we prioritize non-surgical methods, surgery is sometimes necessary for nerve relief and stability:

  • Microdiscectomy: Minimally invasive removal of herniated disc material to relieve sciatica (leg pain).
  • Laminectomy (Decompression): Creating space for the spinal cord to treat spinal stenosis.
  • Spinal Fusion: Stabilizing the spine using screws and rods for conditions like Spondylolisthesis.
  • Vertebroplasty: Cement injection for osteoporotic compression fractures to provide immediate pain relief.

Arthroscopy & Sports Medicine

Keyhole surgery allows for diagnosis and treatment of joint problems through tiny incisions, leading to faster recovery and less pain:

  • ACL Reconstruction: Rebuilding the torn anterior cruciate ligament in the knee using grafts, essential for athletes.
  • Meniscus Repair: Suturing torn cartilage in the knee to preserve joint function.
  • Rotator Cuff Repair: Fixing torn tendons in the shoulder to restore arm strength and movement.
  • Shoulder Instability Surgery: Treating recurrent shoulder dislocations (Bankart repair).

General Orthopedic Surgeries

We also handle a wide range of other surgical procedures including Carpal Tunnel Release, Trigger Finger Release, Tendon Repairs, and Infection Control (debridement) for bone and joint infections.
